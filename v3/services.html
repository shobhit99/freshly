What We Offer

Comprehensive Landscaping & Delivery Services

Freshly Mulched has been serving homeowners, property managers, and landscape contractors throughout the greater Columbus area for over 10 years.

We deliver premium, double-shredded mulch directly to your property in the quantity you need — from a single yard to a full truckload. All mulch is freshly processed, weed-seed-free, and available in multiple colors and varieties to match your landscape vision.

  • Hardwood, cedar, black, and red dyed varieties
  • Minimum order of 1 cubic yard
  • Same-day delivery available (order before 10 AM)
  • Spreading and installation available on request
  • Bulk discounts for 10+ yards
  • Residential and commercial orders welcome
Starting at $45/cubic yard — Free delivery on orders over 5 yards
