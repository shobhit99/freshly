Got it — for a service business + product sales website, the prompt should focus on credibility, service presentation, and conversion, not SaaS features.

Here is a much better prompt tailored for service businesses (this works very well with GPT, Claude, v0, Lovable, Cursor, etc.).

Prompt: Redesign a Service Business Website Using Inspiration

You are an expert UI/UX designer and modern web designer specializing in high-converting service business websites.

Your task is to redesign an existing website using design inspiration from several reference websites.

The goal is to create a modern, clean, and premium website that clearly communicates the business services while also including a product catalog page to sell products online.

Inputs
Existing Website (to redesign)

https://freshlymulched.com/

Reference Websites (design inspiration)

https://sota.bulkdelivery.app/catalog

https://gomulch.com/collections/bulk

https://www.ohiomulch.com/collections/mulch

https://ulandscapeit.bulkdelivery.app/product/dyed-brown-mulch/delivery

https://americanmulchproducers.com/mulch/

https://mulchsource-mn.com/shop/
https://www.rockhardlandscapesupply.com/products/mulch-soil/bulk-mulch

Use these references to understand:

Layout patterns

Visual hierarchy

Typography

Section structure

Navigation

UI components

Overall design style

Do not copy them directly. Use them only for inspiration.

Goal of the Redesign

Create a professional, modern, and conversion-focused website that:

• Clearly explains the business services
• Builds trust and credibility
• Makes it easy for visitors to contact or request a quote
• Includes a clean product catalog page where users can browse and purchase products
• Looks modern, premium, and visually engaging

Design Principles

The redesigned website should be:

• Clean and minimal
• Easy to navigate
• Visually structured with strong hierarchy
• Mobile-friendly and responsive
• Conversion-focused
• Professional and trustworthy

Use modern patterns such as:

• large hero sections
• clear CTAs
• service cards
• visual sections with images/icons
• testimonials and trust signals

Pages to Design
1. Homepage

Sections may include:

Hero section with headline + CTA

About the company

Overview of services

Why choose us

Featured products

Testimonials / reviews

Process / how it works

Call-to-action

Contact section

2. Services Page

Each service should be clearly explained with:

service overview

benefits

visuals/icons

process steps

CTA (book consultation / request quote)

3. Products Page

Design a clean product catalog layout including:

product grid

product cards

filters or categories

product detail page

add-to-cart / purchase CTA

The product section should feel like a modern ecommerce layout.

4. About Page

Include:

company story

mission / values

team section

credibility signals

5. Contact Page

Include:

contact form

business information

map

phone/email

CTA for inquiries
